Unit 2

For my Unit 2 literature review, I selected the topic "Use of Information Systems to Transform UK Healthcare: A Literature Review of AI Systems". My interest lies in understanding why so many implementations of AI in healthcare fail to deliver their intended outcomes, despite high expectations and significant investment. I noticed that while there is enthusiasm about the potential of AI systems, the research exploring the practical barriers and limited success of these implementations remains relatively underdeveloped. To support my academic writing, I read Boza’s (2022) guide on how to structure and conduct a literature review. I also reviewed Greenhalgh et al. (2009), who explore the challenges of introducing IT systems into the NHS, and Topol (2019), who provides a comprehensive analysis of how AI might reshape the future of healthcare in the UK. These resources helped me frame my research question and critically assess the current state of evidence in this field.

References:

  • Boza, T. (2022) How to Write a Literature Review: Six Steps to Get You from Start to Finish. Available at: https://www.scribbr.com/dissertation/literature-review/ (Accessed: 21 July 2025).
  • Greenhalgh, T., Stramer, K., Bratan, T., Byrne, E., Russell, J. and Potts, H.W.W. (2009) ‘The devil’s in the detail: Final report of the independent evaluation of the Summary Care Record and HealthSpace programmes’, University College London.
  • Topol, E. (2019) The Topol Review: Preparing the healthcare workforce to deliver the digital future. NHS Health Education England. Available at: https://topol.hee.nhs.uk/ (Accessed: 15 July 2025).
